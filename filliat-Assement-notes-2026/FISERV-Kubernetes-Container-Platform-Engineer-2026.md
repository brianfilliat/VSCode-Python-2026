# Kubernetes Container Platform Engineer (PaaS & CaaS)

## Job Purpose
The Kubernetes Container Platform Engineer builds, operates, and automates container platform services (PaaS & CaaS) to enable self-service delivery for thousands of developers and DevOps teams. You will manage platform lifecycle, onboarding, upgrades, observability, and integrations across public cloud and on-prem infrastructure.

## Principal Duties & Responsibilities
- Design, implement and operate container service platforms with strong CI/CD integrations.
- Automate platform operations; eliminate manual repetition through reliable tooling and IaC.
- Operate VMware/Pivotal BOSH, Tanzu/Pivotal platforms, and Kubernetes distributions (TKG/TKGI, OpenShift, PKS).
- Deploy and support Kubernetes on public IaaS providers (Azure, AWS, GCP, Alibaba).
- Troubleshoot, diagnose and resolve production issues; own customer support requests and status.
- Implement identity, access, and certificate management integrations.
- Design and operate logging, monitoring and observability stacks (Prometheus/Grafana/Alertmanager, Fluentd/Fluentbit, Splunk, Wavefront, AppDynamics).
- Produce maintainable, reusable automation and platform APIs (Swagger/OpenAPI where applicable).
- Write and peer-review documentation and knowledgebase articles; participate in on-call rotation.

## Required Skills & Experience
- Proficiency in a programming or scripting language (Go preferred; Python strongly desired).
- Hands-on experience building and maintaining container platforms (PAS/TAS, TKGI/TKG, OpenShift).
- Experience deploying/supporting Kubernetes on at least one public IaaS.
- Strong UNIX/Linux and Windows systems administration experience.
- Infrastructure-as-code and automation experience (Concourse, Jenkins, Salt, Fabric, Octopus Deploy, Terraform, etc.).
- Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines and Git-based workflows (GitHub/GitLab).
- Solid networking fundamentals (TCP/IP, subnetting, routing, DNS, load balancing).
- Experience with observability, logging and policy engines (Prometheus, Grafana, Fluentd, OPA).
- Experience with virtualization (VMware/vSphere) and distributed systems concepts.

## Basic Qualifications
- 4-year college degree + 3 years relevant experience OR advanced degree + 4 years proven experience.
- Minimum 2 years’ experience building and maintaining container platforms.
- Operations or systems administration background with experience managing large-scale infrastructure.

## Preferred / Nice-to-have
- NSX / NSX-T, Panorama, network virtualization experience.
- Experience with .NET Core and .NET Framework workloads.
- Experience with TKG on vSphere 7.
- Experience with Open Policy Agent (OPA) or other policy engines.
- Experience with certificate lifecycle automation and IAM integrations.

## Personal Attributes
- Obsessed with automation and repeatability; avoids manual one-off work.
- Meticulous and risk-aware; balances caution with delivery speed.
- Strong communicator and team player; experience in agile environments.

## How to Apply
Send your resume and a brief note describing your relevant platform experience to the hiring contact. Include examples of automation or platform projects (links to repos or docs preferred).
